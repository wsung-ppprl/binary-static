Binary.com ICO FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ICO?

An ‘Initial Coin Offering’ (or ‘ICO’ for short) is a fundraising method that’s relatively similar to an Initial Public Offering (IPO). In an ICO, cryptocurrency such as ‘coloured coins’ or ‘Ethereum tokens’ are issued instead of shares.

Why is Binary.com performing an ICO?

Binary.com is a well-established company founded in 1999. The growth of our business led us to consider an IPO, as is the case with most successful companies. However, we believe that stock exchanges will be gradually replaced by cryptocurrency exchanges in the coming decade, and IPOs will be gradually replaced by ICOs. This is why we decided to perform an ICO instead of an IPO.

How will the ICO work?

Binary.com will offer a number of blockchain tokens to investors. These tokens may be issued in various forms on different blockchains, such as ‘coloured coins’ on the Bitcoin blockchain, and ‘ERC20’ tokens on the Ethereum blockchain.

Regardless of the type of token, each token will provide you with two rights:

  1. The right to receive payments equivalent to dividends paid on Binary Group Ltd. Ordinary Shares
  2. The right to convert tokens into Ordinary Shares

How will the tokens be offered?

The tokens will be offered over a period of one month. The exact date of the ICO is yet to be determined, but we are aiming for the fourth quarter of 2017. The ICO price and auction method will be announced in due course.

Who can purchase the tokens?

Tokens will only be offered to Binary.com clients who have a Real Money account, and who have completed our account authentication process.

Is the ICO available in all jurisdictions?

The ICO will be restricted in a number of jurisdictions to qualified or professional investors, in accordance with the rules and regulations of such jurisdictions.

How can I purchase your tokens?

You’ll be able to purchase our tokens using the funds in your Binary.com account. We will soon be introducing native BTC (Bitcoin) and ETH (Ethereum) account facilities to enable your participation in the ICO.

How can I learn more about ICOs and cryptocurrencies?

To learn more about Bitcoin, we recommend that you open a Bitcoin wallet with Blockchain and purchase bitcoins using a local exchange in your country.

To learn more about Ethereum, we recommend that you install the Metamask plugin for Chrome, which will allow you to convert your Bitcoin into Ether.

To learn how to trade cryptocurrency assets, we recommend that you open an account with US-based cryptocurrency exchange, POLONIEX.
